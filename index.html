How It Works

Our three-step process to optimize your EV charging network

1

Data Collection & Integration

We integrate with your existing charging stations and collect historical usage data, traffic patterns, weather information, and demographic data. Our platform also processes real-time inputs from IoT sensors at each station.

2

AI Analysis & Prediction

Our machine learning models process the collected data to identify patterns and predict future demand. We use advanced algorithms including ARIMA and LSTM neural networks for accurate forecasting at different time horizons.

3

Optimization & Implementation

The platform provides actionable insights for station placement, dynamic pricing adjustments, and load balancing. These optimizations are automatically implemented or presented as recommendations for your team to execute.
